
CIVIT Dataset: Horizontal-parallax-only densely-sampled light-fields


*** Dataset description ***

The dataset includes pre-rectified horizontal-parallax multi-perspective images of 3D scenes.

Dataset specifications:
  - Format: raw 8-bit R, G, B color channels.
  - Resolution: 1280×720 pixels
  - Images stored in PNG format
  - Number of densely sampled images per dataset: 193 camera views
  - The disparity range between adjacent views is 1 pixel at most


*** Dataset info ***

This dataset is provided by the Centre for Immersive Visual Technologies (CIVIT), Tampere University, Finland. 

The dataset is available at: http://www.civit.fi. 

If you use this dataset in your research, please refer to the following paper: 
S. Moreschini, F. Gama, R. Bregovic, A. Gotchev, "CIVIT Dataset: Horizontal-parallax-only densely-sampled light-fields," in Proc. European Light Field Imaging Workshop 2019
